Institutional Profile
About SRM Valliammai Engineering College

SRM Valliammai Engineering College (Autonomous), established in 1999 and located at Kattankulathur near Chennai, is a premier institution under the SRM Group. Affiliated to Anna University and approved by AICTE, it holds NAAC A-grade and NBA accreditation along with ISO 9001:2015 certification. Spread over 13.35 acres, the campus offers well-equipped classrooms, advanced laboratories, a spacious library, and hostel facilities. The college is sponsored by the Valliammai Society, founded by Dr. T. R. Paarivendhar in honour of his mother, Tmt. R. Valliammai, whose nurturing ideals remain the moral cornerstone of the institution's mission. Offering eleven undergraduate and eight postgraduate programmes — supported by over 300 qualified faculty — the college maintains a strong placement record, attracting recruiters such as TCS, Cognizant, Accenture, and IBM.

About the Department

The Department of English is committed to imparting quality education and enhancing student communication through innovative, learner-centred pedagogy. Students are trained in Listening, Speaking, Reading, and Writing skills, with particular attention to students from regional-medium backgrounds. The department maintains a modern language laboratory and regularly organises workshops, FDPs, literary events, and skill-based programmes to enrich academic engagement across the institution.

About the FDP

This five-day FDP addresses the evolving needs of English educators by introducing innovative, technology-integrated pedagogical methods. Sessions will cover evolving assessment patterns, research writing and publication, creative content development, practical applications of AI in language teaching, and active learning models such as flipped classrooms — equipping participants with both theoretical foundations and hands-on, immediately applicable tools to transform their classrooms and research outputs.
