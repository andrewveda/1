About SRM Valliammai Engineering College

SRM Valliammai Engineering College (Autonomous), established in 1999, is a premier institution under the SRM Group of Educational Institutions. Located at Kattankulathur near Chennai, the institution is committed to academic excellence, innovation, and holistic student development.


The college offers state-of-the-art infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, a modern library, and an academically vibrant environment that supports teaching, research, and professional growth.


With a strong emphasis on ethical values, employability skills, and learner-centred education, the institution continues to empower future professionals across disciplines.

About the Department of English

The Department of English aims to enhance communication skills, critical thinking, and language proficiency among students through innovative pedagogical practices and learner-centred methodologies.


The department actively organizes workshops, FDPs, literary events, and skill-based programmes to enrich academic engagement and professional development among educators and learners.

About the FDP

The proposed National Level Faculty Development Programme titled “Communication, Critical Thinking and Classroom Engagement” aims to strengthen essential teaching competencies required in contemporary English Language Teaching (ELT).


The FDP focuses on communication effectiveness, critical thinking strategies, interactive classroom engagement, learner-centred pedagogies, and innovative instructional practices.


The programme also serves as a continuation of the successful national-level FDP conducted by the Department during the previous semester, which witnessed enthusiastic participation from educators across India.

Objectives of the FDP

Communication Skills

Enhance effective communication skills required for dynamic and learner-centred English language classrooms.

Critical Thinking

Equip educators with strategies that promote analytical and reflective learning among students.

Pedagogical Innovation

Familiarize participants with contemporary pedagogical practices and interactive teaching methodologies.

Classroom Engagement

Introduce innovative classroom engagement techniques that encourage active participation and collaborative learning.
